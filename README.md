 Sephora Beauty Retail Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes Sephora's retail data to uncover insights into product performance, customer engagement, revenue, pricing strategy, and brand performance. This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow, including data cleaning, transformation, DAX calculations, visualization, and business recommendations.

---

 Project Overview

This project was developed to showcase Business Intelligence and Data Analytics skills using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

 Objectives

- Analyze Sephora's retail performance.
- Track revenue and customer engagement.
- Identify top-performing brands and product categories.
- Compare product pricing segments.
- Measure customer satisfaction through ratings and reviews.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

---

 Key Insights

- Sephora offers over **8,000 products** from **302 brands**.
- Products have received over **4 million reviews**.
- Total customer "Loves" exceed **248 million**.
- Total revenue exceeds **10 Billion**.
- Makeup is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Skincare has the largest product portfolio.
- Luxury products generate the highest revenue.
- Dior, Charlotte Tilbury, Fenty Beauty, TOM FORD, and NARS are the leading brands.
- The average customer rating is **4.19**, indicating high customer satisfaction.

---

 Business Recommendations

- Expand the Makeup category.
- Increase investment in Luxury products.
- Promote underperforming categories.
- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing brands.
- Leverage customer reviews and Loves for targeted marketing.
